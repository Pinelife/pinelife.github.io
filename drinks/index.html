Drinks from Hell & Recipes for Disaster

Moscow Goatfuck Recipe
  • Lime wedge
  • 1 cup vodka
  • Tablespoon fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 cup ginger beer
  • (Pro tip: it's just a regular Moscow Mule but with four times the vodka.)
White Bronco Simpson
  • 1 cup white wine
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • Must be consumed in a car
Bloody Simpson
  • 1 cup red wine
  • 1/2 cup orange juice - this could be swapped out in favour of red cordial if you'd prefer a red-flavoured drink to an orange one
  • Really just a very poor man's sangria
Brown Simpson
  • 1 cup of any goon of your choosing
  • 1/2 cup chocolate milk
The Embalmer
  • 1 cup vodka
  • Entire jar of green olives, less 1 cup of brine
  • Combine the vodka with the brine and olives in the jar
  • Serve room temperature with a fork and a Salada as garnish
