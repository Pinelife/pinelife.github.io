When Twitter launched a decade later, she felt herself inexorably drawn to the platform: after all, she was an internationally beloved author, year after year she outsold the Bible, world leaders read her books to their children every night, she was the most popular single mum since Princess Diana - surely people would want to know what she thought about foreign politics and the latest scandal surrounding YouTube personalities.
